About Me

My Journey

I'm Amaan Khan, a Hyderabad-based Product Designer with a passion for crafting impactful, human-centered products. My journey into design began with a fascination for how technology shapes our lives and a desire to make those interactions more intuitive and delightful.

Over the years, I've had the privilege to work on a diverse range of projects, from mobile applications to complex trading platforms, collaborating with cross-functional teams to bring ideas to life. My approach is rooted in understanding user needs, iterating rapidly, and always striving for elegant solutions to complex problems.

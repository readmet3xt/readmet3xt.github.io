KoinBasket: From MVP to Market Leader

Fintech Mobile App Design System UX Strategy

Introduction

As Founding Designer, I led the strategy and design of KoinBasket’s MVP, simplifying crypto investing. My core challenge was to demystify a complex market, making it accessible and engaging. Following its success, as Senior UX Designer, I led a rebrand, a sophisticated Live Trading Experience, and the innovative “BitBuddy” influencer platform.

Phase 1: The MVP

How can we design a platform that makes diversified crypto investing simple, secure, and engaging?

As the solo designer, I adopted a lean, agile approach, focusing on Critical User Journey Mapping and Iterative Prototyping. The solution included curated crypto "baskets" to simplify choices, a risk-free Demo Mode, and secure non-custodial exchange integration for user control.

Phase 2: Rebrand & Live Launch

The decision to evolve stemmed from the need to elevate brand perception, meet user demand for real trading, and foster community. As Senior UX Designer, I mentored a junior designer, evolved the design system, and led iterative design on major new features.

Learnings & Impact

  • Focus Delivers Value: A clear value proposition resonated strongly in a complex market.
  • Trust is Paramount in Fintech: The non-custodial model was critical in gaining user confidence.
  • Ecosystems Add Value: Features like “BitBuddy” that foster community transform a product into a dynamic ecosystem.

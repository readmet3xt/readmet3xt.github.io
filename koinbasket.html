Case Study: KoinBasket

KoinBasket: From MVP to Market Leader

Fintech Mobile App Design Design System UX Strategy Rebranding

Introduction: The Journey of KoinBasket

As Founding Designer, I led the strategy and design of KoinBasket’s MVP, simplifying crypto investing through curated baskets and secure exchange integration. My core challenge was to demystify a complex market, making it accessible and engaging.

Following the MVP's success, the next imperative was to evolve the platform for a maturing user base. As Senior UX Designer, I managed a junior designer and led pivotal initiatives: a comprehensive rebrand, a sophisticated Live Trading Experience, and the innovative “BitBuddy” influencer platform. This case study details this journey of transformation, focused on enhancing trust, delivering a sophisticated trading experience, and fostering a vibrant community.

Phase 1: The MVP - Laying the Foundation

The Strategic Challenge: Lowering Barriers to Crypto Adoption

Our primary objective was to address key user pain points and market gaps:

  • Overcoming “Analysis Paralysis”: Simplifying the overwhelming choice of individual cryptocurrencies.
  • Mitigating Investment Risk: Offering instant diversification through thematic baskets.
  • Ensuring Security & User Control: Leveraging users’ trusted exchange accounts for all transactions and asset custody. This non-custodial approach was a cornerstone of our trust strategy.
  • Delivering Simplicity & Speed: Catering to users who value straightforward, efficient investment processes.
Our guiding problem statement became: How can we design a platform that makes diversified crypto investing exceptionally simple, demonstrably secure, and genuinely engaging, thereby lowering the barrier to entry and fostering immediate user trust?

Design Leadership & MVP Strategy

As the solo founding designer, I adopted a lean, agile approach, prioritizing features that delivered core user value. My strategic design process focused on Critical User Journey Mapping, Iterative Prototyping (from wireframes to high-fidelity UI for both web and mobile), and Strategic Feature Inclusion like demo trading and rewards to encourage learning and growth.

The Solution: Key MVP Innovations

The KoinBasket MVP was architected to provide a seamless experience. Key features included curated crypto "baskets" to simplify investment choices, a risk-free Demo Mode, secure non-custodial exchange integration for user control, and engagement features like DIY Baskets and Crypto Fantasy Leagues.

Phase 2: The Evolution - Rebrand & Live Launch

The Strategic Imperative: Why Evolve?

The decision to rebrand and introduce advanced live features stemmed from the need to elevate brand perception for a more discerning audience, meet user demand for real trading, and foster a community through our "BitBuddy" influencer platform. We also envisioned an immersive "Live Trading Experience" where users could watch live analysis and trade simultaneously.

Our new mission: How can we transform KoinBasket into a market-leading platform that cultivates a trusted ecosystem of users, experts, and curated content—all wrapped in a refreshed, highly credible brand identity?

Design Leadership & Process: Orchestrating Evolution

As Senior UX Designer, my role was to define the UX vision, mentor our Junior UX Designer, and collaborate with stakeholders. The process involved deep-dive research, evolving the design system for the rebrand, and iterative design of major new features for web and mobile.

The Solution: A Rebranded KoinBasket with Powerful Products

The evolved platform delivered a more sophisticated and trustworthy experience. Key deliverables included:

  • A Fresh, Modern Rebrand: A cleaner, lighter aesthetic was introduced to feel more professional and transparent.
  • The Immersive Live Trading Experience: A flagship dashboard where users can watch live trading streams, view market data, and execute trades in one place.
  • The “BitBuddy” Influencer & Content Ecosystem: A dedicated portal for influencers (“Bitpals”) to create content and curate baskets, and for users to follow their expert insights.
  • Enhanced & Consistent Mobile Experience: The mobile app was fully updated with the new brand identity and seamless access to all live features.

Strategic Learnings & Impact Across the Journey

This foundational project was rich with learnings that shaped KoinBasket’s trajectory:

  • Focus Delivers Value: A clear value proposition—simplified, diversified crypto investing—resonated strongly in a complex market.
  • Trust is Paramount in Fintech: The non-custodial model and transparent design were critical in gaining user confidence.
  • A Rebrand is a Strategic Business Initiative: It must be deeply aligned with evolving business goals, target audience needs, and the desired market positioning.
  • Integrating Content and Commerce Creates Powerful Synergies: The Live Trading Experience highlighted the potential of merging educational content directly with transactional capabilities.
  • Building an Ecosystem Adds Exponential Value: Features like “BitBuddy” that foster community and leverage expert knowledge can transform a product into a dynamic and sticky ecosystem.
  • The UX of Trust in Live Financial Products is Paramount: Every design decision in a live trading environment must prioritize security, transparency, clarity, and user control.

Conclusion: KoinBasket Reimagined

The KoinBasket journey, from a focused MVP to a feature-rich live platform, demonstrates a powerful evolution driven by user-centric design. As Founding Designer and later Senior UX Designer, I led the strategic design efforts that validated the core concept and later transformed the platform into a mature, competitive, and deeply engaging product.

By strategically focusing on enhancing user trust, delivering sophisticated yet accessible functionality, and building a strong foundation for community engagement, we successfully positioned KoinBasket to empower a new generation of crypto investors and solidify its place as a leader in the space.
